Chess Principles

Home Page

  1. Develop your pieces quickly.
  2. Try to control the centre.
  3. Pieces first, pawns second.
  4. Maintain a good pawn structure.
  5. Castle early.
  6. Do not move the same piece twice in the opening phase.
  7. Do not get your queen out too early in the opening phase.
  8. A knight in the rim is dim (grim).
  9. A bishop pair is better than a knight pair or a knight and a bishop.
  10. Centalise your king in the end game.
